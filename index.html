Arcade Game

Classic Arcade Game

Instructions

Use your arrow keys on your keyboard to move the player through the game. Try and not to collide with the bugs or you will lose 50 points.

Game Objective

Get to the water at the top of the screen to collect your gems. Collect 3 gems and win the game.

Each games starts with 3 lives.

Game Over!

Oh no, you are out of lives!
